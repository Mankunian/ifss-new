Travel Arrangements

Parents are responsible for travel and insurance arrangements to Astana, Kazakhstan. All students will be met by a member of IFSS staff at Nazarbayev International Airport and accompanied to the campus of the Nazarbayev University.

Accommodation and Meal

Students will live on Nazarbayev University campus. All rooms have beds, desks and closet space. Residential equipment, medical office, study rooms, laundry facilities, mini markets, vending machines, barbershop and canteens are available in the residential halls. Meals are served at university cafeteria 3 times per day

Who can apply?

All students aged 13-18 years old and returning to school.


How to apply and Deadlines?

Apply online on our website www.ifss.kz or email at info@ifss.kz. Application deadline is May 30, 2018.

Is financial aid available?

Yes, we offer merit-based financial aid.

Do I need a visa?

Kazakhstan is visa free for citizens of more than 60 states, who are planning to visit Kazakhstan up to 30 days. For more details please email us at info@ifss.kz.

Questions?

Our admissions office will be happy to help with any queries or issues that you may have regarding the application process.
