About us

IFSS offers 13 to 18 year olds a balanced summer experience of academia, fun and culture, where students can learn within an encouraging and supportive environment while having fun and making new friends from around the world.

About IFSS

IFSS was created with one clear mission in mind: To create an ecosystem for people to unleash their potential. As such, we provide a well balanced, supervised summer program where students choose academic and elective classes to suit their needs and wider interests. We offer our students the opportunity to have the summer adventure of a lifetime where the focus is on personal safety and wellbeing, quality individual instruction and a unique international experience.

The location of our summer school has been carefully chosen for its safe, stimulating and inspirational campus. Young and modern Nazarbayev University allows students to experience life at this exclusive university, within a secure environment. By attending IFSS, students have the opportunity to experience the Astana – heart of the Eurasia and home of pink flamingos.

IFSS endeavor to deliver the highest quality programs in the safest location led by an inspirational team. We welcome students from all over the World and create an international family where friendships span the world and last long beyond the summer»

What Makes IFSS Different?

  • We welcome students from all over the world
  • Beautiful, safe and world renowned locations
  • Satisfaction rate is 87%

  • IFSS gives students the edge when applying to university

Our Team

We strive for high-quality program and have selected a strong team consisting of The Princeton Review Certified Trainers, students and alumni of top Kazakhstan and U.S. schools, such as Nazarbayev University, Eurasian National University, Stanford, Duke, MIT, Williams College, Pennsylvania StateUniversity and University of Wisconsin-Madison. There is a staff student ratio of 1:12 and classes are intentionally kept small in order to provide individual attention and personalized instruction.

As well as providing high quality academic standards for our summer students, our teachers are also dormitory residents and are backed up by counselors who act as role models and provide pastoral care. The success of our summer programs rests on the dedication and enthusiasm of our teachers and counselors and their common goal of making IFSS a truly unique and exciting experience for every student.

We also have an amazing full time head office team who spend the year making sure that IFSS really is The Experience of a Lifetime.
