Students at ISSOS will enjoy a wide range of exciting evening programs.

Evening Entertainment

All students at ISSOS take part in each evening program and it is a great chance to hang out with friends, discover a new talent or learn a new skill. Some evening programs are ISSOS clan events and are designed to foster friendships, good sportsmanship and teamwork. We make sure all evening programs are full of fun and laughter.
