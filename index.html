Accommodation

Accommodation for Summer School participants is provided by Nazarbayev University. All participants are assigned to state-of-the-art quadruple (4 students) rooms with free wifi. All rooms are equipped with furniture and closet space. Also available: several canteens, 24-hour medical support, study rooms, mini markets, vending machines and barber shop.
