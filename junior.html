Alanya, Turkey


Alanya is a holiday resort located on the south coasts of Turkey with its popular and still-breathing ancient history. The city’s unique, natural and blue flagged beaches lie along the south shores.

Exclusive Summer School Program


National School of Physics and Math invites students of grades 3-6 to take part in the IFSS Junior “EnglishUP” Summer School on the Mediterranean coast. The unique program includes intensive English language classes with a native speaker, vocabulary enrichment, art classes, and entertaining math courses.

APPLY NOW

Accommodation

Accommodation for Summer School participants is provided by 4-star Enki Hotel. All participants are assigned to state-of-the-art triple (3 students) rooms with free wifi. All rooms are equipped with furniture and closet space. Also available: several cafes, swimming pool, 24-hour medical support, mini markets, and vending machines. Hotel is just 300 m away from the beach.

Cultural Experience and Activities


We always remember that the health of children is very important and included in the program a daily two-hour exercise in the fresh air. Students have the opportunity to look through the centuries-old history of Alanya and learn about Turkish culture and traditions.

Dates and Cost


This year’s program is scheduled from June 15 to June 28, 2019. Cost of the summer school is 684,000 KZT. It covers: flight costs, tuition, accommodation, meals, course materials and equipment; trips and excursions; evening activities and cultural events; airport transfers from and to Antalya International Airport (AYT), Antalya, Turkey. There is an enrollment fee of 50% of the cost. Please note that your place at IFSS will not be confirmed until the enrolment fee has been received.

Accommodation and Meals


Students will live in 4-star, alcohol-free Enki Hotel. All rooms have beds, desks and closet space. Swimming pool, medical office, laundry facilities, mini markets, vending machines, and cafes are available in the hotel. Meals are served at hotel restaurant 3 times per day. Application, Deadlines, and Financial Aid

Do I need a visa?


Turkey is visa free for citizens of more than 70 countries and e-visas available for more than 40 countries, who are planning to visit Turkey for different periods of time. For more details please email us at info@ifss.kz.
