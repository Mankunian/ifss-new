Exclusive Summer School Program


We have developed our summer school so that our students have an opportunity to take exceptional academic courses, combined with a wide range of self-development activities. We strive for high-quality program and have selected a strong team consisting of The Princeton Review Certified Trainers, students and alumni of top Kazakhstan and U.S. schools, such as Nazarbayev University, Eurasian National University, Arizona State University, Duke, MIT, Williams College, Pennsylvania State University and University of Wisconsin-Madison.

Accommodation


Accommodation for Summer School participants is provided by Vefa High School. All participants are assigned to state-of-the-art rooms with free Wi-Fi. All rooms are equipped with furniture. Also available: several canteens, 24-hour medical support, study rooms, mini markets, and vending machines.

Vefa High School


Vefa Anatolian High School is one of the oldest and internationally renowned high school of Turkey.It was the first non-military school. Campus is located in the heart of the Istanbul just in 5 minutes walk from famous Mosque Suleymanie Camii.

Dates and Cost


This year’s program is scheduled from June 16 to July 6, 2019. Cost of the summer school is 2,300 USD. It covers: tuition, accommodation, meals, course materials and equipment; trips and excursions; evening activities and cultural events; airport transfers from and to Istanbul International Airport (IST), Istanbul, Turkey. There is an enrollment fee of 50% of the cost. Please note that your place at IFSS will not be confirmed until the enrolment fee has been received.

Travel Arrangements


Parents of international students (non-Kazakhstan citizens) are responsible for travel and insurance arrangements to Istanbul, Turkey. All students will be met by a member of IFSS staff at Istanbul International Airport and accompanied to the campus of the Summer School.

Accommodation and Meals


Students will live on VEFA High School campus. All rooms have beds, desks and closet space. Residential equipment, medical office, study rooms, laundry facilities, mini markets, vending machines, and canteens are available in the residential halls. Meals are served at school cafeteria 3 times per day.

Application, Deadlines, and Financial Aid


All students aged 13-18 years old and returning to school are eligible to apply. Application deadline for IFSS 2019 Senior Program is April 30, 2019. All applicants are automatically considered for merit-based financial aid.

Do I need a visa?


Turkey is visa free for citizens of more than 70 countries and e-visas available for more than 40 countries, who are planning to visit Turkey for different periods of time. For more details please email us at info@ifss.kz.
